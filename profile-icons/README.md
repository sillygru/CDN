Player profile thumbnail icons linked by their IDs from [player_thumbnails](https://api.brawlify.com/game/csv_logic/player_thumbnails), [the official API](https://developer.brawlstars.com/), or [BrawlAPI](https://brawlapi.com/).

# Standards

## regular
Profile icons shown with their in-game borders. Typically 200x200. All assets must be compressed.

> [!NOTE]
> Some icons are not available right after an update release from our sources, so it takes a little while to find and upload them. New icons will temporarily be redirected to the [`Unknown.png`](/profile-icons/regular/Unknown.png) placeholder. On websites a placeholder is better than a `404 Not Found` error, as it impacts SEO and browsing experience.
